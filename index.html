Affogato

“I’m getting emotional just looking at this.
This is a gift for my boyfriend for our three year anniversary,
on April 5. Unfortunately we cannot celebrate with
the pandemic happening. Our spirits have been low,
and we’ve experienced a lot of stress financially and health wise.
I cannot thank you enough for this piece. .

It’s more than I could have ever imagined. Each image
is a special memory we have, and to look at this brings
them all back. Cannot wait to be able to frame
this and to admire it for years to come.”

- Kianna, recent Portrai Me customer

QUARANTINE EDITION

A few weeks ago we decided to offer free
shipping for a limited time due to COVID-19
and its impact on the economy. We know it’s only
a small gesture - but it’s a small thing
we can do to continue to help make these pieces
available to those who want them. .

Marisol

Very excited to share the technicolor dream world
of Marisol Muro through this Portrai Me curated by her
and for her! .

Marisol is a designer, artist, and an extremely
unique tastemaker. You might recognize the Salvation
Mountain drawing we posted yesterday. That was just
one piece of Marisol’s incredibly fun and psychedelic
list for her Portrai Me. .

Say their names:⁠

⁠ George Floyd, Breonna Taylor, Ahmaud Arbery,
Philando Castile, Atatiana Jefferson, Eric Reason,⁠
Natasha McKenna, Botham Jean, Walter Scott,
Bettie Jones, Tamir Rice, Michael Brown,
Dominique Clayton,⁠ Eric Garner, Trayvon Martin,
Tanisha Anderson…⁠

⁠ These are just a few names of many beautiful lives
which have been taken by police brutality and
blatant racism in our country.
THIS NEEDS TO STOP.⁠ ⁠
